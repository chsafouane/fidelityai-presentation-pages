Product Overview

What FidelityAI is

FidelityAI is a claim triage platform for insurance teams that need faster, defensible outcomes. It brings evidence ingestion, interview context, and policy guidance into one operational workspace.

Use Cases

What teams use it for

Prioritize suspicious claims, equip adjusters with consistent evidence summaries, and improve handoffs between intake, investigation, and resolution.

How It Works

Three steps to production-ready claim triage

Step 1

Ingest Claim Evidence

Capture claim forms, supporting documents, images, and transcripts in one guided intake flow.

Step 2

Run Multi-Agent Review

Specialized AI agents evaluate coverage fit, inconsistencies, fraud indicators, and policy alignment in parallel.

Step 3

Deliver Explainable Decision Support

Analysts receive transparent summaries, clear rationale, and confidence-backed recommendations.
